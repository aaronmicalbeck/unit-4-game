Collect the Crystals!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game begins, the crystal values will change.
